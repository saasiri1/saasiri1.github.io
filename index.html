Sultan Asiri, PhD

About me:

I am a Ph.D. student in the computer science department at the University of Alabama. My research interests are Phishing detection, advertisement attacks, and applying Natural Language Processing. I am a member of Dr. Xiao's lab (W4Net). I will work with Dr. Xiao on phishing attacks and detection. In fall 2014, I received my undergraduate degree from King Khaled University in Computer Information Systems. In spring 2019, I received my Master's Degree from Gannon University in information analytics. I enjoy playing video games and watching soccer games.
